Overview

This hack's content has high fighting spirit and filled with challenging levels and JoJo references.


Features

  • plot related to a manic addiction to literature.
  • many levels with updated graphics and music, each with its own challenges.
  • fights at the end of each level. You will face either a group of enemies, or 1 of 7 completely original bosses.
  • and, of course, FUN.


Travel to new lands as Mario's alter ego and put an end to the Deo organization!
